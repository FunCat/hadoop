Elena Mirolioubova arrived in France in 1998 and began law studies as well as following courses on painting and photography. 
In 2004 she settles down in Paris and decides to focus only on art after her first exposition in Moscow under the name of Elena Elbe. 
Sigmund Freud's Dreams Museum and the Vladimir Nabokov Museum in Saint Petersburg have hosted several of her exhibitions. 
Her installations, paintings and photographs are regularly exhibited in Russia, Europe and United States.
